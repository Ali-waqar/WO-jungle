Canopy, a powerful AI trained on your data

Canopy’s AI uses historical data to learn how your machines function under all operating conditions. We are deployed in various industries, helping to detect failures ahead of time, reduce machine downtime and increase performance.

Trusted by customers around the globe

Why choose Canopy?

Remote deployment

Our technology doesn't require any hardware installation and is remotely deployed.

Investigations to the sensor level

Canopy allows you to explore developing issues to the sensor level. Advanced visualisations and tools show the machine state in different ways, empowering you to find out exactly what is abnormal in your machines.

Fully Unsupervised Learning

Without requiring annotated failures, Canopy learns what normal behaviour is and can extrapolate what abnormal is. We don’t train for specific failure types. If the abnormality is in your data, Canopy will find it, no matter the cause.

Contextual Alarms are Meaningful Alarms

Canopy’s alarms are dynamic and contextual, and can detect abnormalities in any operation condition. No more unnecessary alarms.

A Powerful AI trained on your data
